In this latest release of Firecamp v3.2.0, the profile, workspace, and org management are now integrated within the app, no sub-domain redirects are required. Easily manage user profiles, workspaces, and organizations directly from Firecamp. Effortlessly handle invitations to workspaces and organizations within the application. Access and accept invitations without leaving Firecamp.


## What has been improved?
Following components/features are now integrated within the app. previously they were available from app.firecamp.dev different domain. 

1. → Profile Management
2. → Workspace Management
3. → Organization Management
4. → Invitations Management
5. → Org/Workspace Members List
6. → Accept Invitations within the app
